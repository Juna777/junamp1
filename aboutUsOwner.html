    About the Owner



    "We recognize the importance of our mission and vision in addressing the environmental concerns associated with the fashion industry. The fast fashion industry has been a major contributor to pollution, resource depletion, and unethical labor practices. By promoting sustainable fashion through the sale of second-hand items, we aim to reduce the demand for new clothing production and consumption.
    Through our mission and vision, we aspire to create a shift in the way people perceive and engage with fashion. We want to empower individuals to make conscious choices that not only benefit their wallets but also have a positive impact on the planet. We believe that fashion should not come at the expense of the environment or the well-being of garment workers.
    As we continue to grow and expand our business, we are dedicated to staying at the forefront of sustainable fashion. We actively seek opportunities to collaborate with other like-minded businesses and organizations to further promote the adoption of circular fashion practices and reduce textile waste.
    One of our main focus is to provide customers with a chance to refresh their fashion choices while embracing the sustainable and affordable aspect of thrift shopping. It highlights the allure of finding unique and fashionable items while being conscious of budget and the environment. Together let's revamp our style, embrace thrift!"

    -Clarisse
