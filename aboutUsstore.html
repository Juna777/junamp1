    About Us

    Clarisze Ukay Bale is a reputable wholesale business based in Cebu City. Established in 2020, they specialize in providing second-hand dresses, specifically denim dresses, maxi/mini dresses, and silky sleepwear dresses. With a primary focus on helping starting and struggling online sellers succeed, Clarisze Ukay Bale aims to ensure that their resellers can generate a significant profit.
    Clarisze Ukay Bale understands the challenges faced by online sellers, especially in sourcing high-quality products at affordable prices. Their commitment to excellence is evident in their meticulous selection process, where each dress goes through thorough inspection to ensure its quality. By offering trendy and fashionable second-hand dresses, Clarisze Ukay Bale equips resellers with the inventory needed to cater to the demands of their customers.
    As a reliable wholesale business, Clarisze Ukay Bale not only provides premium products but also ensures competitive pricing, giving resellers the opportunity to maximize their profit margin. By offering attractive pricing options, Clarisze Ukay Bale supports the growth and success of online sellers, providing them with a viable business model.

    Additionally, Clarisze Ukay Bale prioritizes customer satisfaction by providing exceptional service and support. The knowledgeable team is always available to address any inquiries, offer assistance, and provide guidance to resellers. This commitment to excellent customer service fosters strong relationships and trust between Clarisze Ukay Bale and their valued resellers.

    Whether you are an aspiring online seller or an established business looking to diversify your inventory, Clarisze Ukay Bale is the ideal partner to help you achieve your goals. Their extensive selection of second-hand denim dresses, maxi/mini dresses, and silky sleepwear dresses not only meets the fashion needs of a wide range of customers but also ensures profitability for resellers.


    Mission/Vision

    At our UKay wholesale business, our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable second-hand items to our customers while promoting sustainability and reducing waste. We aim to be a reliable source for retailers looking to offer unique and eco-friendly options to their customers. We envision a world where everyone has access to fashionable and sustainable clothing options, without compromising their budget or the environment. We strive to be a force for positive change in the fashion industry, driving innovation and inspiring others to make more informed and sustainable choices


    Values

    At our UKay wholesale business, we are committed to the following values:

    • 1. Sustainability: We strive to reduce textile waste by sourcing high-quality second-hand items that are still in excellent condition. By giving these items a second life, we contribute to minimizing the harmful environmental impact caused by the fashion industry.
    • 2. Affordability: We believe that everyone deserves access to fashionable and quality clothing, regardless of their budget. Our goal is to offer our customers affordable options that don't sacrifice style or quality.
    • 3. Customer satisfaction: We prioritize the needs and satisfaction of our customers by ensuring that we provide a wide selection of items that cater to different tastes and preferences. We aim to create a positive shopping experience by offering exceptional customer service and maintaining a hassle-free return and exchange policy.
    • 4. Trust and transparency: We maintain a high level of transparency in our business practices, from the sourcing of our second-hand items to our pricing and operations. We believe in building trust with our customers by providing accurate descriptions and images of our products, and by being open and honest about any imperfections or flaws.
    • 5. Community engagement: We are committed to giving back to the community and making a positive impact. We actively seek partnerships with local charities and organizations that support sustainable fashion and textile recycling. By collaborating with these organizations, we aim to contribute to the wellbeing of the community and increase awareness about the importance of reducing textile waste.
    • 6. Continuous improvement: We are dedicated to constantly improving our business processes and practices. We regularly review our operations to identify areas for enhancement and strive for greater efficiency and effectiveness. This includes staying up-to-date with the latest fashion trends, investing in technology to streamline our operations, and seeking feedback from our customers to ensure their needs are met.

    Things to Know

    When shopping for Ukay Ukay clothes, check the overall condition of the clothes. Look for any signs of damage, such as tears, stains, or missing buttons. Ensure that the fabric is still in good condition and retains its original quality.Though second-hand, make sure the clothes are clean and free from unpleasant odors. If possible, you can wash or sanitize them before wearing.
    Determine if the price of the Ukay Ukay clothes aligns with their condition and quality. While Ukay Ukay items tend to be more affordable, it's still important to assess whether the price matches the value.
